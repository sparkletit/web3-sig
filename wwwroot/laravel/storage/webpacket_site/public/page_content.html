World of Fairy is a web3.0 game on Polygon. It's in early stages but more will join us to improve it and experience the beauty of the Web3 world.

The game features

Planting

Buy seeds, grow them into crops, and sell the harvest to earn FLT.

Gatherning

Purchasing tools for mining allows you to collect various resources and platform tokens WOF.

Livestock

Livestock can produce a continuous supply of animal products, which the fairies highly depend on.

NFT

Use the resources within the game to create NFTs, which can increase your output.

market

Instantly trade with other users, collect resources or sell resources to earn FLT.

Entertainment

We offer various competitive activities that are open to everyone and conducted in a fair and transparent manner, with high rewards for participants.

Co-creation

The game content is mostly initiated and created by the community, and all participants work together to create internet value.

shared profit

Participants will receive a share of the project's profits, and we are actually implementing it, something that nobody has really done before.

Create a Wallet.

Frequently Asked Questions
Does this game need to be downloaded to a mobile or PC?

No, you don't need to download the game to your phone or PC. You can directly play the game on the website using your mobile or computer browser.

Do I need to buy tokens or NFTs to participate in the game?

You don't need to buy our tokens and NFTs to play the game. You can earn FLT by minting coins or NFTs during your idle time and trading them on the market. However, you need to purchase an Origin Island to enter the game. The relative price of the Origin Island is very low, and the cost you pay will be used for the team's basic research and development. Thank you very much for your support.

What makes this game different from other Web3 blockchain games?

Like other blockchain games, you will own the ownership of your data on the internet, but "World of Fairy" is not like most projects that use Web2 games or designs applied to Web3. Instead, it starts from scratch and builds this parallel world together with users.

Is there a high learning curve for the game?

In the Beta version, the Origin Island is just a simple simulation game, and you don't need to spend a lot of time or learning costs. You can simply collect profits at regular intervals. As our team grows and more people join, we will create a more diverse and fun platform.
